Enterprise Build Service Engineer

  • Virtualization knowledge (KVM/XEN) and/or Cloud experience (EC2, Openstack, Azure) a plus
  • Familiarity with version control systems (git), databases (SQL)
  • Team player
  • Excellent verbal, written and interpersonal communication skills
  • Autonomous and results oriented work style
  • User and customer focus
  • Addiction to applying your skills to challenging problems
